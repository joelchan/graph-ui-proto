Problem Graph for Our Project

Make a graph of your own project! Click here to see some examples.

How to make a graph:

  • To create a new node: right click anywhere and select "add node".
  • To edit the label for a node: right click on a node and select "edit label".
  • To draw an edge between nodes:
    • Hover over a node. An 'anchor' point will appear. Click and drag from the anchor point to the desired target node.
    • Click "draw on" button at the bottom of the page and click and drag between nodes.
  • To delete a node or edge, right click on the element and select "remove".

Other actions:

  • To move a node, select it by clicking on it, then click and drag to desired location.
  • To pan the canvas, left-click and drag.
  • To zoom in/out on the canvas, scroll up/down.
  • To select multiple nodes, hold the 'option' key and drag to create a selection box.
